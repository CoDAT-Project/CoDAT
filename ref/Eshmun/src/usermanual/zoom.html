Search and Zoom

Search

You can search for a state by typing its name into the search box (top right corner), then hitting enter (or tab), the search will find the state with the given name, and scroll to make it in the top left corner of the panel. This will only search inside the current open structure (not the whole Concurrent Kripke structure for example).


Zoom

You zoom in and out to view the graph better, you could use the zoom sliding bar (top left corner), slide left to zoom out, and right to zoom in.

You can also type the desired ratio into the zoom box right next to the zoom sliding bar. Just type the number (no %) and hit enter. Number less than 100 means zoom out, and greater than 100 means zoom in.s

You could also zoom in / out by using the View Menu, or by right clicking on a free point, then choosing the appropriate zoom action, this will increase / decrease the zoom by 1/3 of the current ratio. You could also use Ctrl + +, and Ctrl + -.

You can reset zoom at any time, by going to the View Menu -> reset zoom (Ctrl + 0).
